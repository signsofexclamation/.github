# privacy policy

*whatpeoplethink* does not know who you are.

there is no account, no name, no email. when you first open the app it makes a random id and keeps it on your device — it is not tied to your identity and tells us nothing about you.

when you answer, the option you picked is sent to the server with that random id, so the app can count the results. that is the only thing stored. 

if you turn on notifications, apple gives us a token to send you the daily question. turn them off and it is not used.

no analytics. no tracking. no ads. nothing is sold or shared.

if you have questions, open an issue on this repository.
